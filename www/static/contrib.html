Contributors
The below artists contributed audio to this project. If you would also like to contribute audio, contact nynex at hydrogen project point com or call (xxx) xxx-xxxx. By contributing audio, you implicitly allow the Republic of Nynex to remix your work and retransmit it in that form.

Z. D. Smith is a member of NYC-based metal band Flaming Tusk. His solo electronic album; Pneumatic, Psychic, Sarkic; is now part of the Republic.

Radio Scotvoid, a DJ and chiptune artist living in Salem, Massachusetts, has allowed the use of his entire catalog, including his side projects My Brother Daniel and Small Radio. Scott has a weekly radio program on WMWM 91.7 FM and hosts a monthly event at Salem's Gulu-Gulu Café.

Unfields is a set of field recordings released by the Unfoundsound netlabel offshoot of Philadelphia's Foundsound Records.

The Republic of Nynex - demonstrations
The Republic of Nynex is designed to serve as a bridge between the online world and the physical one.

The Republic of Nynex will be part of the 2010 Megapolis Festival in Baltimore, from May 14 to May 16. More information will be available soon.

The Republic (or something like it) will be taking part in S.C.E.N.E. 2010 in the fall.

From here, you can follow the software's development, read the progress blog, follow the project on Twitter, listen to the project's creations on SoundCloud, join the announcement mailing list, read more detailed information about the project, become a Facebook fan, or see information on audio contributors. Soon, you will be able contribute your own audio recordings, listen to new compositions and rate them, listen to previous generations of compositions, and read about the Republic of Nynex's creator.

This site looks best if you have the Futura typeface installed.

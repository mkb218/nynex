The Republic of Nynex.
The Republic of Nynex is an audio art installation that uses genetic programming to create audio compositions using audience-submitted raw audio and a Yamaha FS1r synthesizer.
From here, you can follow the software's development, follow its progress, read more detailed information about the project, or see information on audio contributors. Soon, you will be able contribute your own audio recordings, listen to new compositions and rate them, listen to previous generations of compositions, read on the Republic of Nynex's creator, read more detailed information about the project, post this project to social bookmarking sites (e.g. Digg, Delicio.us, Facebook, Twitter), and read where in-person demonstrations will be occurring.
